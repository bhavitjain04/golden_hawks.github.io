About Us

We are the team "Golden Hawks" representing Biomedical Engineering Department of Shri GS Institute of Technology & Science hereby presenting our major project work "Anaemia Detection using Smartphone Images". We came up with this idea considering affordability & accessibility issues of healthcare services in remote areas of India. We moved forward with this idea of non-invasive detection of Anaemia to help the people of remote areas to quickly detect the issue of Anaemia and as a result, save them for any other major diseases or fatigueness.
